---
title: "Nausea - Jean-Paul Sartre"
description: "Jean-Paul Sartre's first and most famous novel"
---

Sartre's _Nausea_ follows Antoine Roquentin, a melancholic and socially isolated intellectual living in Bouville while he works on a biography of a historical figure. He finds himself affeceted by a persistent nausea which, while he struggles to understand it at first, we might nowadays simply call depression. The book is powerful and surprisingly easy to read, and I recommend it for anyone interested in questioning their own feelings and views of the world !
