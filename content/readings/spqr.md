---
title: "SPQR (Histoire de l'ancienne Rome) - Mary Beard"
description: "Everything you need to know about Rome"
---

Mary Beard's _SPQR_ meticulously describes the history of Rome, sharing details that go far beyond the usual tales of emperors and military conquests. Beard takes you through the city's rise from a small settlement to a vast empire, but what makes it special is how she focuses on the everyday lives of ordinary Romans (the slaves, merchants, and citizens you rarely hear about !). If you have ever wondered what Rome was actually like beyond the textbook, this is the book for you !
